BRAND STYLE GUIDE — TechVision Inc.

VOICE & TONE:
- Professional but conversational — write like a knowledgeable friend, not a textbook
- Use active voice, keep sentences concise
- Address the reader directly with "you" and "your"
- Be confident but not arrogant — show, don't just tell

FORMATTING:
- Use clear headings and subheadings (H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections)
- Keep paragraphs to 3-4 sentences max
- Use bullet points for lists of 3+ items
- Include a compelling introduction that hooks the reader in the first sentence
- End with a clear call-to-action or key takeaway

TERMINOLOGY:
- Say "AI-powered" not "artificial intelligence powered"
- Say "team members" not "employees"
- Say "solution" not "product" when referring to our offerings
- Say "insights" not "data" when talking about analytics output
- Avoid jargon unless necessary — always explain technical terms

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
- Hook → Context → Main Points → Examples → Takeaway
- Every claim should be supported with data, examples, or expert quotes
- Include at least one real-world example or case study
- Target reading level: 8th grade (clear and accessible)

BRAND VALUES TO REFLECT:
- Innovation — we push boundaries
- Transparency — we explain our thinking
- Empowerment — we help readers take action
- Community — we're in this together
